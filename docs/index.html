        Data source: SEC EDGAR 13F-HR filings. Current prices as of 2026-04-24.
        Methodology: 13Fs are filed quarterly with a ~45-day lag and show long U.S. equity and option positions only — they don't show short common-stock positions, fixed-income, non-U.S. listings, or private holdings. "Value" and shares are as reported. "Q-end Px" is derived from value ÷ shares. "Est. entry" uses the quarterly mean daily close for the first quarter the position appeared — a proxy for VWAP. "Est. return" compares current price to the entry proxy and does NOT reflect actual fills or costs paid. Returns on option rows show % change in underlying reference price, not option P&L.
